Trent Borman is graduate research assistant at Penn State University. His research focuses on the characterization of reliability and failure in PZT piezoelectric thin films.

At North Carolina State University he explored the effects of alloying on the formation of titanium nitride to be used as a solar cell contact material, the ferroelectric properties of orthorhombic hafnium oxide, and the synthesis of high entropy oxides.

More information about Trent can be found on his LinkedIn page as well as in his CV. He welcomes opportunities to try different scientific fields, and travel around the world.
