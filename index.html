Trent Borman is a senior in materials engineering at Iowa State University. Upon graduation he intends to go to graduate school for a doctorate in materials science and engineering.

At North Carolina State University he explored the effects of alloying on the formation of titanium nitride to be used as a solar cell contact material, the ferroelectric properties of cubic hafnium oxide, and the synthesis of high entropy oxides.

More information about Trent can be found on his LinkedIn page as well as in his Resume. He welcomes opportunities to try different scientific fields, and travel around the world.
